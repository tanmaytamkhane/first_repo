Featured Equipment

    1.Tractor - Buy/Rent

    50 HP tractors are versatile for small to medium-sized farms and are suitable for tasks like plowing, sowing, and harvesting.

    Popular models include the Mahindra 585 DI XP Plus, John Deere 5050 D, Farmtrac 50 Powermaxx, and Sonalika 745 DI III Sikander.

    Key features to consider are engine capacity, transmission type, and fuel efficiency, as these vary by model and brand.

    2.Disc Plough - Buy/Rent

    A disc plough is a suitable steel plough for all types of soil, including hard, dry, and stony conditions. Its design allows it to cut through tough soil,

    open new fields, and handle rocky and rooted areas effectively.

    Other options like the hydraulic reversible plough or chisel plough can also be effective depending on the specific soil conditions

    with models like the manual MB plough being suitable for a variety of soils.

    3.Harvester - Buy/Rent

    A harvester is a versatile machine that performs harvesting, threshing, and sometimes winnowing in a single pass to efficiently harvest crops like wheat, rice, and corn.

    4.Seeders and Sprayers - buy/rent

    A seeder is an agricultural machine used to sow seeds at precise rates and depths in rows, ensuring even distribution for better germination and simultaneous maturation.

    Sprayers are used to apply liquids for various purposes, most commonly in agriculture for pest control, weed control, and fertilizer application.

    5.Irrigation Equipments - buy/rent

    Irrigation equipment refers to tools, machines, and systems used to supply water to crops or plants to help them grow, especially in areas where rainfall is insufficient. These are designed to efficiently distribute water to the fields and conserve water at the same time
